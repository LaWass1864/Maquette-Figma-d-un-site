**MediaCard** — flat surface card for short editorial blocks (the "Le Noble Art" trio). Lifts and grows an accent border on hover.

```jsx
<MediaCard title="Le Noble Art">La boxe amateur est un sport incroyable…</MediaCard>
<MediaCard title="Ses valeurs" accentTitle>Respect, discipline, humilité…</MediaCard>
```

`accentTitle` colours the heading orange.
